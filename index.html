Got Questions?

Here are answers to the most common ones.

What is "no-code" development?

No-code means building websites, apps, and automations using visual tools — drag-and-drop — without writing traditional code. It's faster, cheaper, and powerful for most use cases.

How much does a project cost?

Starter projects begin at ₹2,999. Check our pricing section above for transparent tiers, or DM us for a custom quote — no commitment!

How long does a project take?

Most projects are delivered within 3–7 days. Complex builds may take longer, but we always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Will I be able to edit my site myself?

Absolutely! We use platforms that are easy to manage and walk you through everything so you feel confident.

I'm not technical at all — is that okay?

100%! That's exactly who we built this for. We handle everything technical and explain it all in simple language.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! We offer post-launch support to help with updates, fixes, and new features as your business grows.
